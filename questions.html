Frequently asked questions

Commitment to our clients

HOW CAN I KNOW THE SIZE OF MY RING?

Many of our Rings are adjustable from size 10 to 16, so you will not have any problems. Those that do go by size have a Size Guide in the product's own file. To access it, you just have to click on "What is my size?".

WHAT IS THE SIZE OF THE NECKLACES?

We are very fans of layering (superimposing Necklaces), that is why our Necklaces have different measures. Typically, you will find them 35cm, 38cm, 40cm, or 50cm with a 5cm extension to keep the game going (50cm Necklaces have a 3cm extension). In any case, do not hesitate to consult our product sheet where you can find the exact measurements and a photo so you can see how each Jewel is placed.

WHAT IS THE SIZE OF THE BRACELETS?

Most of them are 14 cm long + 3.5 cm long. But if you have doubts, the exact length can be found on the tab of each Jewel. And if you want to get an idea of how it looks, remember that you can always see it in the second photo of our product sheet.

CAN I ADD MORE CHARACTERS ON MY CUSTOM JEWELERY?

Sentimos decirte que no es posible. En Singularu tenemos muy en cuenta el diseño de nuestras Joyas y por eso limitamos el número de caracteres de cada pieza personalizada en función del tamaño de la misma.
